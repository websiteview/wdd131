ExploreGuatemala

Your guide to discovering Guatemala’s natural and cultural treasures

Site Name

ExploreGuatemala - This name reflects the site's purpose: to help travelers and locals discover Guatemala’s unique destinations—from Lake Atitlán and volcanoes to Mayan ruins and colonial towns. It is memorable, informative, and ideal for a tourism-focused site.

Site Purpose

This website helps visitors plan culturally rich and eco-friendly trips across Guatemala. It offers travel guides, tour booking options, local experiences, and insider tips for exploring responsibly and safely.

Scenarios

  • What are the best Mayan archaeological sites to visit near Guatemala City?
  • How do I safely hike the Acatenango volcano and see Fuego erupt?
